# Getting Started with HubSpot Extension (Beta)

**Welcome to the Container Tracker for HubSpot!**

The HubSpot Extension is currently in **Late Beta**. We are bringing the same powerful container tracking functionality from our Google Workspace Add-on directly into your HubSpot CRM.

## Join the Beta Program

We are actively accepting a limited number of beta testers to help us polish the experience. As a beta tester, you will get early access to features like:

- **Automated Tracking**: Track shipments directly from your HubSpot deals and tickets.
- **Workflow Integration**: Seamlessly update HubSpot records based on container status changes.
- **Centralized Dashboard**: View all your shipments without leaving HubSpot.
